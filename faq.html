Frequently Asked Questions

Working With Beals Advisory

Answers to common questions about our services, engagements, and approach.

How are engagements structured? ▾

Engagements are designed to be flexible and aligned with the needs of each client. Some companies prefer an ongoing advisory relationship, which we typically structure as a monthly retainer that provides consistent access to strategic financial leadership and support.

In other situations, clients engage us on a project basis—for example, for financial modeling, transaction support, operational improvements, or specific strategic initiatives.

Many engagements also begin with a defined project and evolve into an ongoing advisory relationship as the partnership develops.

How is this different from a CPA? ▾

CPA support is essential for compliance and historical reporting.

Fractional CFO support focuses on forward-looking decisions — helping leadership understand what’s possible, what’s sustainable, and what comes next.

Do we need a full-time CFO to benefit from this? ▾

No. Many businesses benefit from executive-level financial leadership before a full-time role is justified.

Fractional support provides that capability in a flexible format.

What stage of business is this best suited for? ▾

Typically:

  • Growing owner-led businesses
  • Companies preparing to hire or expand
  • Businesses navigating margin pressure or cash flow strain
  • Organizations seeking more decision clarity
What problems does this usually solve? ▾

Common outcomes include:

  • Improved visibility into future cash position
  • More confident hiring and investment decisions
  • Better alignment between growth and sustainability
  • Clearer understanding of profitability drivers
Is this operational or strategic? ▾

Both. Financial leadership is most effective when strategy and execution are connected.
