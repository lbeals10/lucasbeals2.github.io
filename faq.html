Frequently Asked Questions

How is this different from a CPA?

CPA support is essential for compliance and historical reporting.

Fractional CFO support focuses on forward-looking decisions — helping leadership understand what’s possible, what’s sustainable, and what comes next.

Do we need a full-time CFO to benefit from this?

No.

Many businesses benefit from executive-level financial leadership before a full-time role is justified.

Fractional support provides that capability in a flexible format.

What stage of business is this best suited for?

Typically:

  • Growing owner-led businesses
  • Companies preparing to hire or expand
  • Businesses navigating margin pressure or cash flow strain
  • Organizations seeking more decision clarity

What problems does this usually solve?

Common outcomes include:

  • Improved visibility into future cash position
  • More confident hiring and investment decisions
  • Better alignment between growth and sustainability
  • Clearer understanding of profitability drivers

Is this operational or strategic?

Both.

Financial leadership is most effective when strategy and execution are connected.
